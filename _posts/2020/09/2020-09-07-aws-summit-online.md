Amazon is hosting their AWS Summit online this year starting on September 29, 2020 at noon Eastern (9am Pacific). You can register for the event by visiting [https://jasong.us/3lOMGO0](https://jasong.us/3lOMGO0). The event will feature a keynote followed with over 50 on-demand sessions featuring AI, big data, containers, serverless, and more! 

For the complete agenda and session list, visit [https://jasong.us/3boORmB](https://jasong.us/3boORmB).
